About

I am a clinical postdoc fellow with Dr. Shayna Rosenbaum at York University, Toronto and Dr. Robin Green at UHN-Toronto Rehab Institute. I completed my professional training in clinical psychology at University College London and my doctoral research was supervised by Dr. John King. I explored the neural correlates of spatial (object location) memory using immersive virtual reality in healthy older adults, Mild Cognitive Impairment and early Alzheimer's disease.
