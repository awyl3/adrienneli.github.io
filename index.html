About

I am a clinical postdoc with Dr. Shayna Rosenbaum at York University, Toronto and Dr. Robin Green at UHN-Toronto Rehab Institute.

I completed my professional training in clinical psychology at University College London. My doctoral research was supervised by Dr. John King. I explored the neural correlates of spatial (object location) memory using immersive virtual reality in healthy older adults and patients with Mild Cognitive Impairment and early Alzheimer's disease.


Current projects

I am currently investigating the influence of individual differences in mental imagery, anxiety and hippocampal function on spatial learning using a VR model of York University, supported by a VISTA postdoctoral fellowship award.
